## üì± How to Write a Review (Student)

### Step 1: Complete a Booking Session
- Book a session with a tutor
- Attend the session
- Wait for the session to be marked as "completed"

### Step 2: Navigate to My Bookings
1. Open the mobile app
2. Login as a student
3. Tap on "Bookings" from the bottom navigation or dashboard
4. Go to the "Completed" tab

### Step 3: Find the Session to Review
- Look for completed sessions
- Sessions that haven't been reviewed will show a **"Write Review"** button
- Sessions already reviewed will show **"Review Submitted"** with a green checkmark

### Step 4: Write Your Review
1. **Tap "Write Review"** button
2. You'll be taken to the Create Review screen
3. **Rate Overall Experience** (1-5 stars) - Required
   - 5 stars = Excellent
   - 4 stars = Very Good
   - 3 stars = Good
   - 2 stars = Fair
   - 1 star = Poor

4. **Write Your Review** (Optional but recommended)
   - Share your experience
   - Minimum 10 characters if you write something
   - Maximum 1000 characters
   - Be honest and constructive

5. **Add Detailed Ratings** (Optional)
   - Toggle "Add detailed ratings"
   - Rate specific aspects:
     - **Communication**: How well did the tutor communicate?
     - **Expertise**: How knowledgeable was the tutor?
     - **Punctuality**: Was the tutor on time?
     - **Helpfulness**: How helpful was the session?

6. **Submit Review**
   - Tap the "Submit Review" button
   - Wait for confirmation
   - You'll see a success message

### Step 5: After Submission
- Your review is immediately visible to other students
- The tutor's average rating updates automatically
- The tutor can see your review and respond
- You can edit your review within 24 hours

## üêõ Troubleshooting

### "Write Review" Button Not Showing
**Possible Reasons:**
1. Session not marked as completed yet
2. You already wrote a review for this booking
3. Booking was cancelled

**Solution:**
- Check booking status is "completed"
- Look for "Review Submitted" indicator
- Contact support if issue persists

### "Failed to Submit Review" Error
**Possible Reasons:**
1. Network connection issue
2. Booking ID invalid
3. Review already exists

**Solution:**
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the bookings list
- Restart the app
- Contact support if error continues

### Can't Edit Review
**Possible Reasons:**
1. More than 24 hours have passed
2. Review was already edited once

**Solution:**
- Reviews can only be edited within 24 hours
- After that, they're permanent
- You can flag inappropriate reviews for moderation
