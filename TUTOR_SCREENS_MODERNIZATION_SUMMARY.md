## Design Philosophy

**Professional & Elegant** - Not colorful like student side

### Color Palette

**Light Mode:**
- Background: #F5F7FA (soft gray)
- Cards: White → #F5F7FA gradient
- Text: #0F3460 (dark blue)
- Accents: #0F3460 → #16213E gradient

**Dark Mode:**
- Background: #1A1A2E (deep navy)
- Cards: #16213E → #0F3460 gradient
- Text: White
- Accents: #6B7FA8 → #8B9DC3 (slate blue gradient)
- Success: #7FA87F (sage green)
- Warning: #D4A574 (warm gold)
