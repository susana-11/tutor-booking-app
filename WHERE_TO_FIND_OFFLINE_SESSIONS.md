## Common Questions

**Q: I don't see offline sessions option when booking?**
A: Make sure the tutor offers "In-Person" sessions. Check their profile for teaching mode.

**Q: Where do I see the meeting location?**
A: Tap on the booking in your Bookings tab. The location and map will be displayed.

**Q: How do I check in?**
A: When you arrive at the location, open the session and tap "Check In". You need to be within 100 meters of the meeting point.

**Q: What if I can't find the offline session?**
