## Color Palettes

### Student (Colorful)
**Light Mode:**
- Background: #F8F9FA
- Primary: #6B46C1 (purple)
- Secondary: #38B2AC (teal)
- Accent: #805AD5 (pink)

**Dark Mode:**
- Background: #1A1A2E
- Cards: Gradient backgrounds
- Text: White
- Accents: Bright gradients

### Tutor (Professional)
**Light Mode:**
- Background: #F5F7FA (soft gray)
- Cards: White → #F5F7FA gradient
- Text: #0F3460 (dark blue)
- Accents: #0F3460 → #16213E gradient

**Dark Mode:**
- Background: #1A1A2E (deep navy)
- Cards: #16213E → #0F3460 gradient
- Text: White
- Accents: #6B7FA8 → #8B9DC3 (slate blue)
